1. Compliance Overview

RecepAI is committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance across all applicable regulations, industry standards, and best practices. Our comprehensive compliance program ensures that our AI receptionist services meet or exceed regulatory requirements while protecting customer data and maintaining service integrity.

2. Data Protection Compliance

2.1 General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

  • Scope: Applies to all EU residents' data
  • Legal Basis: Clear lawful basis for all processing
  • Data Rights: Full support for individual rights
  • DPO: Dedicated Data Protection Officer
  • Breach Notification: 72-hour reporting procedures

2.2 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

  • Consumer Rights: Right to know, delete, and opt-out
  • Data Categories: Clear disclosure of data collection
  • Third-Party Sharing: Transparent sharing practices
  • Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

2.3 Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)

  • Canadian Compliance: Full PIPEDA compliance for Canadian users
  • Consent Management: Meaningful consent procedures
  • Privacy Policies: Clear and accessible privacy information
  • Complaint Handling: Formal complaint resolution process
