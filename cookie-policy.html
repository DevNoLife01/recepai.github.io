Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our services.

2. How We Use Cookies

RecepAI uses cookies for various purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly:

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your session while browsing
  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you logged in to your account
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraudulent activity
  • Load Balancing Cookies: Distribute traffic across our servers

3.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us improve our website performance:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and performance
  • Hotjar: Records user interactions for UX improvements
  • Error Tracking: Identifies and fixes technical issues

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your user experience:

  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language and region settings
  • Theme Cookies: Save your display preferences
  • Form Data: Remember information you've entered in forms

3.4 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used for marketing purposes:

  • Tracking Pixels: Measure ad campaign effectiveness
  • Retargeting Cookies: Show relevant ads on other websites
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable social sharing features

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

4.1 Analytics Services

  • Google Analytics: Web analytics service
  • Mixpanel: Product analytics platform
  • Hotjar: User behavior analytics

4.2 Marketing Services

  • Google Ads: Online advertising platform
  • Facebook Pixel: Social media advertising
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag: Professional network advertising

4.3 Support Services

  • Intercom: Customer support chat
  • Zendesk: Help desk software
  • Calendly: Appointment scheduling

5. Cookie Duration

Cookies have different lifespans:

5.1 Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.

5.2 Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted:

  • Short-term: 24 hours to 30 days
  • Medium-term: 30 days to 1 year
  • Long-term: 1 year to 2 years

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

6.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

6.2 Cookie Consent Manager

Use our cookie consent banner to:

  • Accept or reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Change your preferences at any time

6.3 Opt-Out Links

Opt out of specific tracking services:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Google Ads: Ad Settings
  • Facebook: Ad Preferences

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your experience:

7.1 Essential Cookies

Disabling essential cookies may result in:

  • Inability to log in to your account
  • Loss of shopping cart contents
  • Reduced website functionality
  • Security vulnerabilities

7.2 Non-Essential Cookies

Disabling non-essential cookies may result in:

  • Less personalized experience
  • Repeated requests for preferences
  • Less relevant advertisements
  • Limited analytics insights

8. Mobile App Tracking

Our mobile applications may use similar tracking technologies:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique identifiers for analytics
  • Local Storage: Store preferences and data locally
  • Push Notifications: Send relevant updates
  • Location Data: Provide location-based services

9. International Transfers

Some cookies may transfer data internationally:

  • We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place
  • Data transfers comply with applicable privacy laws
  • Third-party services have their own privacy policies

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in our cookie usage
  • New legal requirements
  • Technology updates
  • Service improvements

We will notify you of significant changes through our website or email.
